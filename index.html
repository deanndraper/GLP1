About Our Program

The transition from GLP-1 receptor agonist medications is a critical phase that requires expert guidance and structured support.

Why Choose Our Weaning Program?

Our GLP-1 receptor agonist weaning program helps individuals safely and effectively discontinue their weight loss medication while maintaining their results and healthy eating patterns.

Unlike simply stopping medication, our approach combines psychological techniques, behavioral conditioning, and medical supervision to ensure a successful transition.

Important: This program begins while you're still taking your medication at full dosage to establish the necessary foundation for a successful transition.

Our Approach Addresses:

  • Fear of weight regain after medication discontinuation
  • Return of hunger cues and managing appetite changes
  • New habit formation and behavior conditioning
  • Sustainable dietary and lifestyle adjustments
  • Addressing and identifying root causes of addiction and self-destructed behaviors that often result in obesity

Our 5-Step Process

Our comprehensive program guides you through each stage of transitioning off GLP-1 medications with expert support at every step.

1

Initial Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of your medication history, weight loss journey, lifestyle factors, and transition goals with our medical team.

2

Personalized Plan

Development of your custom weaning schedule and habit-building protocol based on your unique needs and medical profile.

3

Behavioral Conditioning

Structured therapy sessions to establish healthy patterns while still on medication, including CBT, mindfulness, and optional hypnotherapy.

4

Guided Reduction

Medically supervised gradual decrease in medication dosage with concurrent reinforcement of established behaviors and real-time adjustments.

5

Long-Term Support

Ongoing accountability and guidance to ensure lasting success after complete medication discontinuation.
